Est. 2022 from a house on the east side of Los Angeles, EVAMM Radio Season 1 is 120 hours of continuous mixes from 65 DJs.

Artists were given no rules on style or sound, but instead had the freedom and comfort to show up however they wanted and play music they're passionate about while we rolled video/audio for 3 years. You'll hear a wide range of styles from legendary names to those just starting out and everything in between. The Season 1 web player serves as a radio station snapshot in time of a particular but evolving sound from Queer dance communities across LA.

Thank you to everyone who participated. If you hear music you like and want IDs, start by finding the DJ and tell them you like their mix, then ask them who they're spinning. We encourage connection over music recognition software.
