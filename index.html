Season 1
2023 – 2026

Established in 2022 from a house on the east side of Los Angeles, EVAMM Radio Season 1 is over 120 hours of continuous mixes from over 65 local DJs.

Performances and events are mostly how we experience a DJ's work. But there's another side to it. What they do at home, when they're comfortable, curious, and not performing for anyone but themselves and their friends. EVAMM shifts the focus to that. Take your shoes off, or don't. Just get comfortable.

Artists were given no directives on style or sound. Just free range and the comfort to show up however they wanted. Experimental, casual, or timeless mixes, from legendary names to those just starting out, you'll hear a wide range of perspectives.

Thank you to everyone who participated. If you hear music you like, definitely go check out the DJ.
